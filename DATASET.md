## Dataset: Fish Market Dataset

This dataset contains measurements of several fish species, including:
- `Weight`
- `Length1`
- `Length2`
- `Length3`
- `Height`
- `Width`
- `Species`

It is commonly used for **regression tasks** such as predicting fish weight from physical characteristics.
